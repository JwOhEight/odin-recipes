Chicken Parm

Chicken Parmesan (also called Chicken Parmigiana) is one of the most loved, most popular AND most ordered chicken recipes in the world. BUT often times, chicken comes out too dry, or doesnâ€™t have enough flavour or seasoning. The sauce is bland, or cheddar cheese is used instead of mozzarella, etc.

This recipe has a fair amount of steps but it is worth it once it is finished.

Ingredients List

For Chicken:

    2 eggs
    1 tablespoon minced garlic
    Salt and pepper to season
    3 large chicken breasts (havled horizontally to make 6 filets)
    1 cup Panko breadcumbs
    1/2 cup breadcumbs (Italian or golden)
    1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese
    1 teaspoon garlic or onion powder
    1/2 cup olive oil for frying

For Sauce:

    1 tablespoon olive oil
    1 large onion(chopped)
    2 teaspoons munced garlic
    14 ounces (400 g) tomato puree (Passata)
    Salt and pepper to taste
    1 teaspoon dried Italian herbs
    1 teaspoon sugar (optional)

For Topping:

    8 ounces (250 g) mozzarella chesse sliced or shredded
    1/3 cup fresh shredded parmesan
    2 tablespoons fresh chopped basil or parsley
